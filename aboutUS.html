About Us



GrowGenix: Revolutionizing Growth Hormone Therapy for Children

Our Commitment

At GrowGenix, we are dedicated to enhancing the lives of children with growth deficiencies through our innovative IGF-1 protein (mecasermine) therapy.

Our Process

  1. Research & Hypothesis

    2. We start with a comprehensive review of existing research to identify opportunities for innovation and formulate our hypothesis.

  2. Experimental Design

    3. We design rigorous experimental protocols, ensuring compliance with all safety and ethical standards.

  3. Protein Production

    • Gene Cloning & Expression: We clone the IGF-1 gene and optimize production in a suitable expression system.
    • Purification & Characterization: Our advanced techniques ensure high-purity IGF-1, confirmed through detailed characterization.

  4. In Vitro Testing

    5. We conduct in vitro assays to ensure the bioactivity and stability of IGF-1.

  5. Preclinical Studies

    6. We assess the efficacy, dosing, and safety of IGF-1 in animal models.

  6. Clinical Trials

    • Phase I-III: Our clinical trials rigorously test safety, efficacy, and optimal dosing in children with growth deficiencies.

  7. Regulatory Approval

    8. We compile comprehensive documentation for regulatory submission and continue post-approval monitoring.

  8. Education & Outreach

    9. We educate healthcare providers and patients about our therapy, ensuring widespread awareness and accessibility.
